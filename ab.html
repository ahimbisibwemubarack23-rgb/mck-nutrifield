Our Mission

MCK-NutriField combines geospatial field delineation with nutrition vulnerability insights to guide climate-smart agriculture and inclusive nutrition policy. Our platform processes satellite imagery to map field boundaries, analyzes nutrition data from multiple sources (FS-COR, DERPIn, AGWAA), and provides personalized crop recommendations that optimize both yield and nutritional outcomes. By connecting field-level data with nutrition intelligence, we help farmers make better decisions, policymakers target interventions more effectively, and communities address nutrition gaps through data-driven approaches. Our AI models predict crop performance under different climate scenarios and provide actionable insights to improve food security and nutritional diversity. We're dedicated to helping smallholder farmers optimize their yields while addressing nutrition gaps in vulnerable communities through data-driven approaches.

>Our biggest challenge is processing high-resolution satellite imagery efficiently to detect field boundaries and crop health indicators. The computer vision models require significant GPU resources for training and inference, which is currently cost-prohibitive. Additionally, integrating multiple data sources (geospatial, nutrition, climate) into a cohesive predictive model requires substantial computational resources that exceed our current capacity. We need access to scalable GPU infrastructure to improve model accuracy, reduce processing time, and handle larger geographic areas.
